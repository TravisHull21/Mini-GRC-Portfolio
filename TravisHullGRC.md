This repository demonstrates practical GRC skills through hands-on artifacts developed for fictional or anonymized scenarios. It highlights my ability to design policies, assess risks, map controls to frameworks, and support audit readiness.

### Key Skills Demonstrated
- Policy development and management
- Risk identification, assessment, and mitigation
- Compliance gap analysis and framework mapping (NIST CSF, ISO 27001, SOC 2)
- Third-party risk and incident response support
- Audit evidence tracking and control implementation

### Portfolio Contents

#### 1. Governance
- **Information Security Policy** – High-level policy outlining security objectives, roles, and responsibilities.
- **Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)** – Guidelines for employees on system usage.
- **Access Control Policy** – Rules for provisioning, least privilege, MFA, and RBAC.

#### 2. Risk Management
- **Risk Register** – Excel template with sample risks (likelihood, impact, mitigation, residual risk).
- **Risk Assessment Report** – For a fictional SaaS company (includes threat modeling and prioritization).

#### 3. Compliance
- **NIST CSF 2.0 Gap Assessment** – Checklist and maturity scoring for a small organization.
- **ISO 27001 / SOC 2 Control Mapping** – Spreadsheet showing how controls align across frameworks.
- **Compliance Checklist** – Audit-ready tracker for evidence collection.

#### 4. Other Artifacts
- **Incident Response Playbook** (GRC-focused section) – Roles, escalation, and post-incident review process.
- **Third-Party Risk Assessment Template** – Vendor questionnaire and scoring.
- **Audit Checklist** – Sample for access control or policy compliance review.

### How to Use / View Artifacts
All documents are in the `/artifacts/` folder. Policies are written in Markdown for easy reading and version control. Risk registers and mappings are in Excel format (downloadable).

**Frameworks Referenced**: NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF), ISO 27001, SOC 2, basic GDPR/HIPAA considerations.
