# Access Control Policy

**TechNova Solutions**  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** April 2026

## 1. Purpose
To ensure appropriate access to information systems and data based on business need, while enforcing least privilege and segregation of duties.

## 2. Scope
All systems, applications, data, and users at TechNova.

## 3. Policy Statements
- **Account Management**: Accounts are provisioned/approved by managers + IT. Inactive accounts disabled after 90 days.
- **Least Privilege**: Users receive only the minimum access required for their role.
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**: Required for all remote access, privileged accounts, and cloud services.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Implemented where possible (e.g., admin vs. standard users).
- **Access Reviews**: Quarterly reviews of user access rights.
- **Remote Access**: VPN + MFA required; approved devices only.
- **Password Requirements**: Minimum 12 characters, complexity, no reuse (enforced via tools).

## 4. Responsibilities
- **IT/Security**: Manage technical controls and conduct reviews.
- **Managers**: Approve access requests for their teams.
- **Users**: Safeguard credentials and report lost/stolen devices.

## 5. Enforcement
Non-compliance may lead to access revocation or disciplinary action.

## 6. References
- NIST PR.AA (Identity Management, Authentication, and Access Control)
- ISO 27001 Annex A.5 (Organizational controls) and A.8 (Technological controls)
- SOC 2 CC6 (Logical and Physical Access Controls)

**Review**: Annually or after major system changes.
