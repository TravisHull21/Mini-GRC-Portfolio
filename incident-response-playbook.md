# Incident Response Playbook (GRC-Focused)

**TechNova Solutions** | **Version:** 1.0

## 1. Purpose
Guide response to cybersecurity incidents while ensuring compliance, documentation, and lessons learned.

## 2. Roles
- **Incident Commander**: CISO or delegate
- **Technical Lead**: IT/Security
- **Communications**: Legal/PR
- **Compliance**: Ensure regulatory notifications (e.g., breach laws)

## 3. Phases (NIST RS/RC aligned)
1. **Preparation**: Tools, training, playbook maintenance.
2. **Identification**: Detect and classify (Low/Medium/High severity).
3. **Containment**: Short-term (isolate) and long-term.
4. **Eradication**: Remove threat.
5. **Recovery**: Restore operations, test.
6. **Post-Incident**: Root cause analysis, report, update controls.

## 4. Escalation & Reporting
- High severity: Notify executives within 1 hour.
- Regulatory: Legal determines notification timelines.

## 5. Templates
- Incident Log | Lessons Learned Report

**Testing**: Annual tabletop exercise required.
